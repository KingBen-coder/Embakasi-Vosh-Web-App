About the Church

A faithful community growing together in Christ

Embakasi Vosh Church welcomes every person into worship, discipleship, prayer, and service. Our family gathers to celebrate God's faithfulness and to carry His presence into daily life.

Our Mission

To proclaim Christ, nurture believers, and serve families with love, prayer, and biblical teaching.

Our Vision

To be a Spirit-filled church where worship, transformation, and compassionate outreach shape the community.

Leadership

Our pastors and ministry leaders serve with humility, care, and a commitment to helping every member grow.
